Solar Energy

Solar Power Plant (PLTS) is one of the breakthroughs in saving environmentally friendly electrical energy. Through the implementation of PLTS, PLN's electricity usage will be effectively reduced, more efficient operational costs, and reduction in carbon dioxide emissions.

Electricity is necessary in the movement of industrial wheels and daily life. Almost all work tools and household appliances work on electric power. The need for electricity will increase from time to time. In fact, almost 87% of electricity in Indonesia is generated from fossil energy, such as coal, natural gas, and oil.

It's time to choose the best power generator!

The best driver to cutting electricity cost is to shift to solar panels. Photovoltaic (PV) components will convert light into electrical energy. Apart from being an electricity generator, PV also gives an aesthetic touch to the building. It is a quality investment that promotes efficient and environmentally friendly electricity.

CEE provides services to obtain the application of solar panels for your industrial and residential scope. With a hassle-free investment, our in-house experts will give you assistance tailored to your needs.
